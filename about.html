About Me

I studied Osteopathy at the British School of Osteopathy qualifying in 1987. I then spent some time at the Glasgow Royal Infirmary and Southern General Hospital learning how Orthopedic and Neurologists deal with problems.

My goal is to restore your mobility, keep you pain-free and ensure your rehabilitation is effective, efficient and stress-free. Since I set up my practice in the Orchard Medical Centre back in 1997, I have offered the highest standards of treatment and patient care and am committed to my patients health and long-term wellness.
